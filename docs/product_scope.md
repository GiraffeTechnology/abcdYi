# Product Scope — abcdYi

## What abcdYi Is

abcdYi is Giraffe Agent's first complete B2M industry edition, purpose-built for apparel, textiles, and handicrafts. It delivers a structured, agent-assisted coordination system for managing multi-party supply chains across the full order execution lifecycle — from the moment a buyer submits an inquiry through to final buyer sign-off and supplier memory update.

abcdYi is not a generic workflow tool retrofitted for fashion. Every stage, every participant role, and every approval gate has been designed around the realities of apparel and textile production: irregular order sizes, multi-style runs, tight lead times, fragmented supplier networks, and the need for traceable QC evidence.

abcdYi is not consumer-facing. It does not serve retail consumers directly. It serves professional buyers, designers, brands, traders, merchandisers, and SMEs coordinating production with manufacturers and supply-chain participants.

---

## Who abcdYi Serves

**Independent designers**
Solo or micro-studio designers placing small-batch production orders (100–2,000 pcs per style) with factories they may not have worked with before. They need help structuring requirements, finding capable suppliers, and tracking production without a dedicated merchandising team.

**Small fashion brands**
Brands with seasonal or capsule collections who coordinate across fabric mills, CMT factories, trim suppliers, and logistics providers. Often managing 5–20 styles per season with limited internal operations staff.

**SME apparel and textile businesses**
Small-to-medium manufacturers, cut-and-sew operations, and converting mills that receive orders from multiple buyers simultaneously and need structured handover of requirements, milestones, and QC checkpoints.

**Handicraft producers**
Artisan workshops and handicraft exporters dealing with bespoke, low-volume, high-variation orders where requirement clarity and approval traceability are critical.

## What abcdYi Covers

abcdYi covers the end-to-end order execution workflow:

- **Inquiry intake and requirement extraction** — structured capture of buyer intent, product specs, quantities, lead times, and compliance needs
- **Dynamic order form generation** — adaptive forms that surface missing or ambiguous information before the order advances
- **Participant classification and permission assignment** — identifying which factories, suppliers, and service providers are involved and what each party can see and do
- **Supplier matching and RFQ preparation** — scoring and selecting suppliers, then assembling request-for-quotation packets for human review
- **RFQ dispatch and response normalization** — sending RFQs after approval, collecting supplier responses, and normalizing them into a comparable decision format
- **Order confirmation and production scheduling** — confirming terms, setting production timelines, and establishing milestone checkpoints
- **Milestone monitoring and delay prediction** — tracking production progress and flagging risk of delay before it becomes a problem
- **QC evidence review and delivery handover** — reviewing quality inspection records and managing the physical handover of goods
- **Buyer sign-off and execution record** — closing the order with buyer confirmation and recording the full event history in the Industrial Execution Graph

## Out of Scope

- **Financial transactions and payment processing** — abcdYi does not handle invoicing, payment, or letters of credit
- **ERP or accounting system replacement** — it coordinates execution but does not replace inventory management, bookkeeping, or warehouse management systems
- **Design and product development** — CAD, pattern making, colorway management, and tech pack creation are outside scope
- **Retail or direct-to-consumer operations** — abcdYi operates on the production and supply-chain side, not the sales or merchandising side
- **Customs clearance and trade compliance** — logistics coordination is in scope; customs documentation and compliance filings are not
- **Fabric or material inspection at the mill level** — abcdYi supports QC evidence review for finished or semi-finished goods, not raw material testing
- **Consumer-facing functionality** — abcdYi is not consumer-facing; there is no consumer interface

---

abcdYi is a B2M production coordination product. It coordinates buyer-to-manufacturer workflows and the related supplier, workshop, QC, and logistics participants behind the manufacturer-side execution path.
